Crispy Fried Chicken

Crispy outside, juicy inside — classic fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg chicken pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 cups buttermilk (or milk + 1 tbsp vinegar)
  • Oil for frying

Procedure

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours (overnight best).
  2. Mix flour, salt, pepper, paprika in a bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, pressing to adhere.
  4. Fry in hot oil (175°C / 350°F) until golden and cooked through (internal temp 75°C/165°F).
  5. Drain on a rack; rest 5 minutes before serving.
