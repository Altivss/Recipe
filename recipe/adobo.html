Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino dish — tangy, savory, and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce and garlic for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and brown the chicken pieces on both sides.
  3. Add vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns and a little water. Do not stir until the vinegar boils.
  4. Simmer on low heat, cover, for 25–30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Uncover and reduce sauce to thicken; adjust salt as needed. Serve with rice.
