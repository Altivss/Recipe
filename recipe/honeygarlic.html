Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Sweet and savory pork chops with a sticky honey-garlic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 4 pork chops
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp oil

Procedure

  1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet and cook pork chops until browned and nearly cooked through.
  3. Mix honey, garlic, soy sauce and rice vinegar; pour over chops and simmer until glaze thickens.
  4. Coat chops in glaze and serve hot.
