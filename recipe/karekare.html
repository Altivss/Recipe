Kare-Kare

A rich peanut-based Filipino stew traditionally served with bagoong (shrimp paste).

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail or beef shank, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup peanut butter (smooth)
  • 1/2 cup ground roasted peanuts (optional)
  • 1 banana blossom (optional), sliced
  • 1 bunch bok choy or kangkong
  • 2 tbsp annatto oil (achuete) for color
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste) to serve

Procedure

  1. Boil the meat until tender (1.5–2 hours) with a little salt; reserve broth.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix peanut butter with some hot broth to loosen.
  3. Add peanut mixture to the pot and simmer, stirring until sauce thickens.
  4. Add vegetables (banana blossom, bok choy) and simmer until cooked.
  5. Finish with annatto oil for color and serve with bagoong on the side.
