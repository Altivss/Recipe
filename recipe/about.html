About Rachel's Recipe

Rachel's Recipe shares simple, family-friendly recipes and practical cooking tips designed for everyday home cooks. We focus on approachable meals made with easy-to-find ingredients, so anyone can enjoy cooking without stress. From classic Filipino favorites like Chicken Adobo and Kare-Kare to crispy, savory, and sweet dishes, our recipes are perfect for busy families, beginners, and food lovers alike. Our goal is to make home cooking enjoyable, affordable, and full of flavor—one recipe at a time
