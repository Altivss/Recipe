Welcome to Rachel's Recipe. We are home cooks sharing approachable recipes that bring joy to everyday meals. Our mission is to make cooking simple, tasty, and enjoyable for everyone.

Purpose of this website

Rachel's Recipe aims to preserve and celebrate beloved Filipino home-cooking by providing clear, approachable recipes and practical tips. We focus on everyday family meals — from crispy fried chicken to hearty stews — with step-by-step instructions and helpful variations so both new cooks and experienced home chefs can achieve great results. Our goal is to make authentic flavors accessible, encourage experimentation, and help people create memorable meals to share.
