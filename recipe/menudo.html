Pork Menudo

A comforting Filipino pork stew with tomatoes, potatoes and carrots.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork shoulder, cubed
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup carrots, cubed
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1/4 cup liver (optional), diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Sauté garlic and onion until soft; add pork and brown.
  2. Add tomato sauce and a cup of water; simmer until pork is tender.
  3. Add potatoes and carrots; cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Add raisins and liver (if using) and simmer 5 more minutes; season to taste and serve with rice.
